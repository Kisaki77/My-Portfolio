Cards Game

1 player game

The card game can be played by 1 player vs Computer
It has rules on how a player could win, it uses the 52-card deck.
Each player needs to have 5 cards.
To win the player has to have 4 similar cards with either an Ace, 10, King Queen, Kac, or jocker.
Or they can win with 2 similar cards and 3 similar cards.
or they can win with an Ace + 10 + King Queen + Kac + jocker.
